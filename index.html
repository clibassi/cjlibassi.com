Biography

CJ Libassi is a Senior Policy Analyst at the U.S. Department of Education, Office of the Chief Economist. His research focuses on higher education finance, student financial aid, and college access and affordability. He has published in leading economics journals and authored key policy reports on student borrowing and college pricing trends.
